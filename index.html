Questions

Frequently Asked

The most common questions about our mobile car detailing service in Omaha.

Do you bring your own water and power?

add

Yes. Nixon and Blake arrive fully self-contained with water tanks, generators, vacuums, and every product needed for the detail. You don't need to provide anything — just park the car somewhere we can work, and we handle the rest.

How long does a full detail take?

add

An exterior-only detail typically takes 45–75 minutes depending on vehicle size and condition. An interior detail runs 60–90 minutes. The full Elite Finish (both interior and exterior) usually takes 2–3 hours. Trucks, SUVs, and vans sit at the longer end of the range. We give you a specific time window when we confirm your appointment.

What's the difference between an exterior detail and the Elite Finish?

add

An exterior detail covers the outside of the vehicle only: full foam bath, wheels, tires, tire shine, and streak-free glass. The interior detail is its own service: full vacuum, surface cleaning, seats, and interior glass. The Elite Finish bundles both for the best value — a complete top-to-bottom transformation for $100 on cars, up to $150 on vans.

Which Omaha neighborhoods do you serve?

add

We serve Omaha, Elkhorn, Papillion, Gretna, Bennington, Council Bluffs, and Shelby. The Shelby area is always free for travel. Locations 10–20 miles from Shelby add a $10 travel fee; 20+ miles adds $20. If you're unsure whether we cover your address, give Blake a call at (402) 278-4466 and we'll sort it out.

What payment do you accept?

add

Cash on completion or crypto. We accept Bitcoin (BTC) and Solana (SOL) — after your detail, we'll text you a secure payment link if you'd rather pay with crypto. No card readers, no tips expected, no surprise fees.

Do you detail exotic or classic cars?

add

Absolutely. We offer a Signature Tier for exotics and classics — hand-wash only, pH-neutral products, microfiber-only drying, and specialty upholstery care. These are quoted individually after a brief consultation. Send us a few photos via the booking form or call Blake at (402) 278-4466.
